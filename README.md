# #95 Bullet Point | Fridge Combo

A hyper-minimalist list formatter designed for busy professionals. Clean up messy notes and apply consistent bullet styles instantly. No more manual character deleting or alignment struggles in your business chats.

## 核心 (Core Philosophy)
* **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Paste, format, and copy. That's it.
* **Brain-dead Simple**: Intelligent logic removes existing symbols and replaces them with your chosen style in one click.
* **Zero Friction**: Fixed-view UI and instant button feedback for a smooth, reliable experience on mobile and desktop.

## 機能 (Features)
* **Smart Bullet Conversion**: Instantly switch between Dots, Numbers, Arrows, and Checkboxes.
* **Intelligent Logic**: Automatically detects and removes existing prefixes (`-`, `*`, `1.`, `>`) before applying new styles.
* **One-Tap Clean up**: Remove all empty lines with a single button.
* **Pro-Divider**: Add a clean visual divider `────` to separate your notes.
* **Instant Feedback**: The copy button changes to "Copied!" for clear confirmation without distracting pop-ups.
* **Auto Save**: Your text is saved locally in your browser so you never lose your progress.

## デザイン要件 (Design System)
* **Background**: #fff
* **Main Color**: #333
* **Accent**: #eee / #fafafa (input)
* **Border Radius**: 15px (Strictly applied)
* **Anti-Distraction**: Zero layout shift during operation.

## プライバシー (Privacy)
* No login / No tracking / No ads.
* Browser-only: All processing happens locally on your device.
